# Design Direction - Poppy Joy

## Positioning

Poppy Joy should feel like an editorial brand world, not a promotional webshop.
The visual language is calm, tactile, and warm, with subtle luxury and emotional
depth.

## Core Intent

- Keep the interface clean and minimal, but never cold.
- Prioritize craftsmanship and personality over commercial pressure.
- Let products and materials lead through imagery and spacing.
- Build trust through refinement, consistency, and softness.

## Color Direction

- Use `#FEFDFC` as the main off-white page background.
- Use `#F2EDE3` for containers, cards, soft sections, and product surfaces.
- Use `#242020` for primary text, with opacity for long paragraphs where needed.
- Use `#A3AE65` as the green accent, especially for prices, positive states, and subtle product highlights.
- Use `#82569F` as the purple accent for primary CTAs, shop callouts, links, and brand moments.
- Keep green and purple restrained. The logo, product photography, and fabric designs already carry strong color.

## Typography And Rhythm

- Use serif display moments for storytelling and emotional tone.
- Use modern sans-serif for UI clarity and product readability.
- Maintain generous whitespace and clear vertical rhythm.
- Keep component styling restrained and quiet.

## UI Behavior

- Use subtle CTAs, present but not aggressive.
- Keep product pages informative and tactile: materials, care, detail, context.
- Minimize badge clutter, urgency patterns, or discount-first visuals.
- Maintain continuity between site experience and Shopify checkout handoff.

## Audience Fit

This direction serves design-aware, quality-driven shoppers who value aesthetic
interiors, reusable products, and meaningful rituals over fast consumption.
